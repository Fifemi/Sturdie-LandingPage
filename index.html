Sturdie is a subscription based EdTech platform that
provides curated study plans, career roadmaps, and career
pathways and helps to connect learners to the right
learning platforms for the right courses and subjects.

With Sturdie, you also have the ability to automatically and
seamlessly update your CV/work portfolio every time you
complete a course. This helps you better connect with the
right hiring companies seeking for the right talents in
various career fileds on the platform.
